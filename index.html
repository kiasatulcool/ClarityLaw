Our Philosophy

Law Is Not Just a Profession.
It Is a Responsibility.

Founded in 2009 by Senior Advocate Rajiv Mehra, ClarityLaw Associates was built on a singular conviction: that legal excellence and ethical integrity are not in conflict — they are the same thing.

"The courtroom is where the rule of law either lives or dies. We walk in every day to make sure it lives."

— Rajiv Mehra, Founding Partner

Over fifteen years, we have grown into a full-service firm with a track record across the Delhi High Court, Supreme Court, and commercial tribunals — advising individuals, family offices, startups, and Fortune 500 companies with equal diligence.

Our approach is disciplined, evidence-driven, and transparent. We do not take cases we cannot win. We do not promise what we cannot deliver. We operate on principles, not optics.
